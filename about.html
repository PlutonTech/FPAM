Who We Are

Nigeria's Federal Asset Management Authority

FPAM is the operational arm of the Federal Ministry of Housing & Urban Development, mandated to manage, maintain and preserve Federal Public Asset Maintenance across Nigeria.

Under Presidential Executive Order No. 11, FPAM operates with clear authority to ensure all federal government buildings, housing estates and infrastructure assets are maintained to the highest standards.

Mission & Vision

What Drives Us

Mission: To professionally maintain, manage and optimise Federal Public Asset Maintenance and housing infrastructure for sustainable national development under Executive Order No. 11.

Vision: A Nigeria where every federal building, housing estate and public asset is maintained to world-class standards — safe, functional and enduring for all Nigerians.
