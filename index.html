What We Do

What We Do at FPAMD

We establish a sustainable and standardised maintenance culture for Nigeria’s public assets, ensuring longevity, safety, and efficiency across all government infrastructure.

Asset Maintenance
Maintaining government buildings to world-class standards ensuring longevity and operational efficiency.
Housing Estates
Sustainable management of 250+ federal housing estates across all 36 states and the FCT.
Facility Management
Integrated MEP systems, structural integrity and operational management of federal infrastructure.
Safety & Compliance
Upholding safety standards and regulatory compliance per Presidential Executive Order No. 11.
Asset Inventory
Digital cataloguing and GIS mapping of 2,764+ federal assets for transparent governance.
Revenue Management
Optimising returns from federal asset leases and concessions through accountable frameworks.
Urban Development
Supporting sustainable urban renewal aligned with the Ministry's national development vision.
MDA Coordination
Engaging 31 Ministries, Departments and Agencies for responsible asset stewardship nationwide.

Who We Are

An Online Portal for FPAMD Assets

The Federal Public Assets Maintenance Department (FPAMD) oversees the management, maintenance, and optimisation of public infrastructure across Nigeria.

Our mission is to establish a sustainable and standardised maintenance culture for public assets, ensuring longevity, safety, and efficiency. We enhance service delivery, preserve government investments, and drive economic growth through effective infrastructure management.

Our Mandate

What We Are
Empowered to Do

FPAM derives its mandate from the Federal Government, with powers to act across all asset categories and government agencies.

01
Maintenance & Preservation
Ensure all federal buildings are maintained to standards that preserve their value and extend serviceable lifespan.
02
Regulatory Compliance
Enforce compliance with building codes, safety regulations and government circulars across all 31 MDAs.
03
Revenue Generation
Optimise commercial returns from federal asset rentals, leases and concessions.
04
Asset Inventory
Maintain a comprehensive, up-to-date register of all federal assets including buildings, estates and land.
05
Renovation & Upgrading
Plan and execute renovation projects to modernise federal facilities to contemporary standards.
06
Tenant Welfare
Protect the rights and welfare of federal housing estate residents, ensuring safe and affordable conditions.
